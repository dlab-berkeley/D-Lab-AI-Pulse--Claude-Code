# Data Sources

The economic data files used in this demo come from three sources:

## FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data)
**Website:** https://fred.stlouisfed.org/

The `fred/` folder contains CSV files with various economic indicators:
- `activity.csv` - Economic activity measures
- `external.csv` - External/international indicators
- `financial.csv` - Financial market data
- `housing.csv` - Housing market indicators
- `inflation.csv` - Inflation measures
- `labor.csv` - Labor market statistics
- `sentiment.csv` - Consumer/business sentiment
- `targets.csv` - Policy target variables

## Michigan Survey of Consumers
**Website:** https://data.sca.isr.umich.edu/

The `michigan/` folder contains:
- `michigan_inflation_expectations.xls` - Consumer inflation expectations survey data

## Survey of Professional Forecasters (SPF)
**Website:** https://www.philadelphiafed.org/surveys-and-data/real-time-data-research/survey-of-professional-forecasters

The `spf/` folder contains:
- `spf10years.xlsx` - 10-year ahead forecasts
- `spf_cpi_individual.csv` - Individual forecaster CPI predictions
- `spf_cpi_mean_median.csv` - Mean and median CPI forecasts

## Size Note

These data files are too large to include in the GitHub repository.
Please download from the sources above and place in the appropriate folders.
